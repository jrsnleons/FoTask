What is FoTask?

FoTask is a website dedicated to creating a workspace for students and learners that are struggling to keep thier concentration. It is a place where users are able to get motivated to study through various techniques like todo and pomodoro applications, inspirational quotes and competetiveness through leaderboards. The goal is to create productive and meaningful learning through the various tools provided. This is basically self monitored and no success will be given if the user him/herself does the work. This is a website ought to guide, help and lead to productiveness, not a way to get high grades adn remarks.
